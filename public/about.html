About The Sauce

Welcome to The Sauce — your go-to platform for the latest news and updates from around Maseno, contributed by our community. We strive to bring you a diverse range of stories from different perspectives, focusing on what matters most to you.

Our Mission

We believe in empowering individuals to share their voice. Our mission is to create a space where everyone can contribute, read, and engage with stories that reflect their interests, challenges, and dreams.

Why Choose Us?

  • University news coverage from a diverse set of contributors.
  • Easy submission process for users to share their own stories.
  • Free access to articles and updates — no subscription required.
  • Up-to-date content on the latest trends and issues around Maseno and neighbouring hoods.

Our Values

We are committed to:

  • Integrity: Presenting the truth, as accurately as possible.
  • Creativity: Encouraging fresh, innovative ideas.
  • Community: Building a collaborative environment for everyone.
  • Respect: Valuing each individual's voice and opinion.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are dedicated to making a difference. We work together to curate, edit, and publish articles that matter to you. But, our work doesn’t stop there — the power is in your hands too. Contribute your stories and be a part of this dynamic platform!

Get Involved

If you want to contribute news, articles, or updates, feel free to submit your content. Your voice matters, and we'd love to hear from you!
